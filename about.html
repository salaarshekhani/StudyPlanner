StudyPlanner - Personal Study Dashboard

StudyPlanner is a personal study dashboard designed to help students stay organized, manage subjects, plan timetables, track pomodoro sessions and keep on top of tasks. It gives you a clear revision overview and countdowns for important exam dates.

This website was built as an independent project, using only HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. It stores your data locally in the browser with localStorage, so your study plan stays available without requiring a server or external database.

I am Salaar Adnan Shekhani, a Grade 9 student as of Spring 2026. I am passionate about programming and enjoy building tangible projects like these, constantly working to expand my computer science and web development skills.

StudyPlanner reflects my focus on practical solutions for everyday challenges. It was created to make planning, goal-setting and revision easier for students who want to study smarter and stay on track.
